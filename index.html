How It Works

Four simple steps to ensure your AI systems serve all stakeholders, not just shareholders

1

Upload Your AI Workflow

Currently supporting: n8n (GA), AgentForce (Beta), LangGraph (Coming Soon)

Export your workflow as JSON and upload via CLI or web interface.

2

Philosophical Analysis

Our framework analyzes across three dimensions:

Teleology: What goals is your AI optimizing for?
Epistemology: How reliable are your AI's knowledge sources?
Ontology: How does your AI categorize reality?
3

Stakeholder Balance Scoring

Configure priorities across 6 stakeholder groups:

• Customers
• Employees
• Community
• Environment
• Suppliers
• Shareholders
4

Actionable Recommendations

Get priority-ranked improvements with:

• Implementation code (n8n JSON, workflow configs)
• Expected impact on each stakeholder
• Trade-off analysis
